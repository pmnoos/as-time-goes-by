G'day — I'm glad you found this place.

I started As Time Goes By because I couldn't find a blog that spoke honestly to people like me. Everything online either tried to sell me something or was written by someone who had never actually lived retirement.

So I decided to write it myself. No ads, no products, no condescension — just honest conversations from someone who has been around long enough to know a thing or two.
